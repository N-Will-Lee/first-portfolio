A Little About Me...

I grew up in the quaint little town of Mounatin View California and have always been interested in technology.
I went to UC Santa Cruz and received a chemistry degree (go slugs!) but after working in a commercial lab in Seattle, I decided chemistry was not for me.
Needing a change, I moved to Marin California and for the last two years have been working as a kayak guide. I've always been interested in coding and after years of indecidion I decided to pull the trigger and apply to Galvanize.
I now live in Wash park with my brother and spend any extra time I've got climbing and exploring the mountians!
